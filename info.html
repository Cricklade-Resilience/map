About

This website is designed to help Cricklade Councillors and Resilience Wardens to prepare for, mitigate, and monitor known risks within the town and local area.


Use the Layers menu to browse the available datasets, with more information available when clicking on a feature. Use the Locate button to view your location (turn on GPS).
